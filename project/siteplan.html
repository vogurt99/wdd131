Project Website Plan

Site Name

Jericho's Armory

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a portfolio for my work. It will briefly introduce me and showcase my capabilities, what I can do, and what I have made. The website will also contain my contact information and instructions regarding making a commission.

Scenarios

  • What kind of services do you provide?
  • What kind of cosplay can you make?
  • Where are you based?
  • How can I contact you?
  • It is my first time commissioning a custom piece, where do I begin?
