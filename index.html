Pasig, Philippines

Pasig City is a highly urbanized city in the Philippines, it has a population of over 800,000 people. Primarily residential and industrial, there is a growing commercial and business district in its west thanks to recent development. The city shares its name with the Pasig River, which was an important river for much of early Philippine history. Pasig, meaning "a river that flows into the sea" in the Tagalog language, and the word tagalog itself meaning "river dweller".
